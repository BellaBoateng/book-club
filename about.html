Welcome

Welcome to my library

This space is a collection of books that have stayed with me, challenged me, comforted me, and helped me grow. I wanted to create something that felt personal—part digital bookshelf, part reading journal, and part quiet corner for reflection.

Why I made this

I wanted a space where I could keep track of the books I love and share a little more than just a rating. Books can hold memories, shape how we think, and meet us in different seasons of life. This project lets me bring together reading, design, and storytelling in one place.

Growing with books

Reading has become more than just a hobby for me. Some books have helped me reflect, some have made me feel seen, and others have simply been fun escapes when I needed them. As I grow, the kinds of books I reach for keep changing too, and I like that this library can grow with me.

What you’ll find here

This library holds a mix of romance, literary fiction, manga, reflective reads, and books that just caught me at the right time. Every page is meant to feel personal, thoughtful, and a little cozy.

More than anything, I hope this space feels inviting—like walking into someone’s room, noticing their bookshelf, and getting to know them through what they read.

A little note

This site is still growing, just like my shelf. More reviews, more thoughts, and more books are on the way.
